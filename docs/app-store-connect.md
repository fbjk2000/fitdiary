## Description

FitDiary is a warm, inclusive fitness tracker designed to help you build momentum in a way that feels motivating instead of punishing.

Track workouts, meals, hydration, goals, reminders, and progress in one place. Add a profile photo, invite friends into your circle, and stay connected to the habits that matter most to you.

Whether you are trying to lose weight, improve cardio fitness, recover, build strength, or simply become more consistent, FitDiary gives you a cleaner daily rhythm.

Features include:

- workout logging with edit and delete support
- meal logging with calories and macros
- hydration tracking
- visible streaks and progress summaries
- local reminders for workouts, meals, and water
- goal selection with progress feedback
- profile photos and leaderboard avatars
- friend-circle invites and shared competition
- meal-photo estimation support

FitDiary is built for real life: some days intense, some days gentle, all days valid.

## Review Notes

FitDiary is a health and fitness tracking app focused on workouts, meals, hydration, reminders, goals, and social motivation.

Notes for App Review:

- The app does not require account creation against a live backend service at this stage.
- Meal photo estimation is an assistive convenience feature and not medical advice.
- Reminder permissions are used for local workout, meal, and hydration reminders.
- Photo library and camera permissions are used for profile photos and optional meal-photo estimation.
