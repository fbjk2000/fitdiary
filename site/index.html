Core experience

Everything you need to stay in rhythm.

FitDiary is built for people with different bodies, goals, routines, and starting points. The app avoids macho intensity and instead focuses on clarity, consistency, and care.

Workout logging

Track strength, cardio, and mobility sessions with clean editing, deletion, and streak logic.

Fuel tracking

Log meals with visible units, macro suggestions, and optional meal-photo estimation to reduce friction.

Hydration and reminders

Keep water, workout cues, and meal prompts visible with reminder settings that fit your day.

Goals with context

Choose goals like lose weight, recover, improve cardio fitness, or build strength and see your progress.

Profile identity

Add a profile photo and show up as yourself in your leaderboard, not as a generic placeholder.

Thoughtful design

Dark, warm visuals, strong hierarchy, and keyboard-safe sheets make daily logging calmer and easier.

Community

A friend circle, not a punishment machine.

FitDiary is for people who want motivation without shame. Invite friends, share progress, and build a leaderboard that feels playful, supportive, and real.

"I want a fitness app that helps me stay consistent without making me feel judged."

Invite and share

Visible avatars
