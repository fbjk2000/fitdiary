Support

We’re here to help.

If you run into issues with tracking, reminders, TestFlight access, or meal-photo estimation, send us a note and we will help you out.

Contact channels

  • Email support: support@fitdiary.club
  • General contact: hello@fitdiary.club

Helpful details to include

  • your device type and operating system version
  • the app version or TestFlight build number
  • what you were trying to do
  • a screenshot or screen recording if possible

Common areas we can help with

  • TestFlight invitation and install issues
  • meal-photo analysis not returning an estimate
  • logging, editing, or deleting workouts and meals
  • leaderboard, invites, or friend-circle questions
  • reminders and notification permissions

Feedback

FitDiary is actively evolving. Product feedback, accessibility feedback, and feature ideas are always welcome.
